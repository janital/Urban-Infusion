Fusing ancient traditions and modern times

Humans have been enjoying herbal teas for millennia. We are passionate for natural teas, nurtured by our ancestors. Our experts have carefully selected teas from all regions of the planet – from Japanese teas to herbal wonders of Machu Picchu.

We are passionate about ancient traditions of tea ceremony. Therefore our employees can help you find what you need for you tea ceremony. We have a collection of different teasets and mugs made out of brazillian clay.

We have carefully created a collection of more than 600 different teas from 140 different countries. Come visit us at our office in Amfi Moa, we have 62 most popular teas available here! Our shop is maskfree (just don't tell it to municipality's chief physician.)
